A Presidential Welcome

Welcome to Kappa Theta Pi! We’re excited to share our mission with you. Our fraternity is dedicated to fostering professional growth, building strong alumni connections, encouraging social growth, advancing technological skills, and providing academic support. Join us in shaping the future of engineering leadership.

Our Pillars

The mission of Kappa Theta Pi is built around five key pillars:

Professional Development

Through events like interview training, resume building, one-on-one mentorship, and more, Kappa Theta Pi prepares members for success in technology-related careers. We take pride in developing the tech leaders of the future.

Alumni Connections

Our alumni work in diverse fields from tech giants to startups, consulting, and financial tech, offering a global network of professional connections. They provide invaluable insights and opportunities for current members.

Social Growth

Kappa Theta Pi hosts exclusive social events for members to build lasting friendships, including formals, tailgates, retreats, and more. These events foster a strong sense of community and camaraderie.

Technological Advancement

Members can enhance their technical skills through project teams and workshops, making it easy to expand their expertise. Whether participating in coding challenges or attending tech seminars, growth is continuous.

Academic Support

We foster academic growth by creating a network where members can rely on each other for help in classes and beyond. Study groups, tutoring sessions, and academic workshops ensure that every member excels.
