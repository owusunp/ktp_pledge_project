Welcome to KTP

Kappa Theta Pi is Vanderbilt's only professional technology fraternity, dedicated to developing leaders in the fields of engineering and technology. As the nation’s foremost fraternity for technologists, Kappa Theta Pi fosters professional growth, community service, and a commitment to advancing the tech industry. This mission is exemplified by our members—a diverse and accomplished group of scholars, innovators, athletes, founders, and leaders in various fields. We believe our strength lies in our members' dedication and the support of those who value our vision. On behalf of the entire Kappa Theta Pi community, welcome to our website! Here, you can learn more about our mission, our members, and how to get involved.
